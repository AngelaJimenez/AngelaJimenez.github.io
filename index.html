Teaching Geometry with VR
The motivation behind this project was to analyze the effect of virtual reality on education, specifically on kids learning geometry within the age of 9 to 14 years old. To pursue this purpose, a learning environment was created using the development tool known as Altspace. The teaching process had two parts: the first one let the students observe geometric figures and their corresponding equations, hence, there were at least two examples for each geometric figure which allowed them to visualize different transformations. The second step was highly interactive and consisted of various exercises. Each exercise had three portions: the initial figure, an arrow to point out a transformation and the figure after the transformation. Therefore, the student had to suggest what possible transformation had taken place to change the properties of the figure. The test subjects were six kids with ages varying from 9 to 14 years old. Taking into account their answers and feedback, it was possible to suggest that the usage of virtual reality equipment had a positive impact in their learning process. So Altspace is an exceptional tool for education purposes and it is highly useful for activities that involve social interaction and participation.
FreestAIle
The purpose of this research project was to analyze the limits of the Artificial Intelligence branch specialized in Natural Language Processing (No! this is not a Visual Computing project, this is one I made on AI in order to explore other fields that some day may work on VR). As a result, the idea of this project was to design an algorithm capable of creating a written production made of sentences that follow a logical syntax scheme and a semantics defined by the meaning of each word that is chosen. This algorithm seeks to imitate the process carried out by people who perform the art of freestyle, an activity that consists in producing a type of lyricism characterized by its free and improvised style. Therefore, the program receives a limited amount of information to start off picking a theme which will be used to determine the words that should be selected from a pool of vocabulary, either for their meaning or intent, in order to build a coherent sentence.
